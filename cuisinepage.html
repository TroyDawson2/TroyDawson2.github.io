Italian Cuisine

Italian food is renowned for its fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and culinary traditions. From pasta dishes like spaghetti and lasagna to desserts like tiramisu and gelato, Italy has something to satisfy every person.

Famous Italian Dishes

  • Pizza Margherita
  • Lasagna
  • Tiramisu
  • Risotto
  • Gelato

Dish Information

Dish Key Ingredients Origin
Pizza Margherita Tomato, mozzarella, basil, olive oil Naples, Italy
Lasagna Pasta, ricotta cheese, mozzarella, meat (beef or pork), tomato sauce Emilia-Romagna, Italy
Tiramisu Mascarpone cheese, espresso, ladyfingers, cocoa Veneto, Italy
Risotto Rice, broth, butter, Parmesan cheese, various meats or vegetables North Italy
Gelato Milk, sugar, flavorings (chocolate, fruit, etc.) Italy
